§ The studio · London · Izmir

Studio

World London Media is a premium visual production studio working between London and Izmir. We turn brand ambition into film, frame and feeling.

We are WoLo: a small, deliberate team of directors, producers and artists. Born in London, rooted in Izmir, we build work that crosses borders without losing its voice.

Two cities give us two rhythms: the pace of London and the warmth of Izmir. We move between them so the work always has somewhere true to come from.

We have made films and campaigns for Samsung, Karaca, LC Waikiki, Yeni Raki, Le Cordon Bleu and Barrus London. Different brands, one standard: ideas move, quality shows.

01. Philosophy · What we believe

Ideas move. Quality shows.

We don't chase trends. We craft impressions that endure. Every frame earns its place. Every cut means something. Nothing here is decoration.
