Studio note

Ideas move. Quality shows.

World London Media, London & Izmir

We are a premium visual production studio. We believe design is the act of conveying the right emotion accurately, so we treat animation as a tool, never a goal. Minimalist, cinematic, deliberate.

From content to impact. We craft impressions that endure: your brand, telling its own story, in a language audiences feel before they read. We have made work with Samsung, Karaca, LC Waikiki, Yeni Raki, Le Cordon Bleu and Barrus London.

Six disciplines, one studio, two cities. If you have a story worth telling well, we would love to hear it.

Process

Four phases.
One picture at a time.

PHASE 01

Brief & concept

We listen, then translate the brief into a concept: references, tone, a clear creative direction approved before any camera moves.

PHASE 02

Pre-production

Casting, locations, crew, schedule and storyboards. Everything is planned and signed off so the shoot runs clean.

PHASE 03

Production

Film, 3D and drone, shot to a cinematic standard. Small precise crews, the right light, no wasted frames.

PHASE 04

Post & delivery

Edit, grade, sound and titles. Delivered in every format you need, on time, ready to publish.
