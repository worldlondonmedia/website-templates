Who we are

A studio between
two cities.

World London Media, WoLo, is a premium visual production studio working between London and Izmir.

We bring brand films, advertising, 3D visualization, corporate film, commercial production and drone cinematography under one roof, with a single standard of craft across every frame.

London gives us reach and discipline. Izmir gives us light, pace and warmth. Together they let us move quickly without losing the patience a good frame demands.

Our philosophy

Ideas move.
Quality shows.

We are minimalist and cinematic by instinct. We trust whitespace, choose typography that means something, and treat animation as a tool, never a goal. For us, design is one thing: conveying the right emotion, accurately.

How we think

Restraint is a
creative decision.

We start from the emotion, not the effect. The right feeling, conveyed accurately, is the whole brief.

That is why we cut more than we add. We let whitespace breathe, let a single frame hold weight, and use motion only where it carries meaning. A spinning logo is not direction.

The result is work that feels inevitable: nothing missing, nothing spare. Your brand, telling its own story.
