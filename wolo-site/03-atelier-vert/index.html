❦ Of the Method ❦

Four movements,
one rhythm.

❦ ❦ ❦

  • i.

    The brief

    A conversation about the brand, the audience, and the feeling we are chasing. The brief writes itself once we know the room and the people.

  • ii.

    The concept

    References, treatment, palette, casting, locations and schedule, pinned to the wall and approved before any camera leaves its case.

  • iii.

    The production

    Small crews, long lights, real rooms. On set or in the air, we move with intent because mistakes get expensive in post.

  • iv.

    The delivery

    Edit, grade, sound, and final masters. Cut for screen and feed, delivered on time, archived for the long run.
