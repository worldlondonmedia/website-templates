/ 03 The Studio

A studio of light, patience and craft.

World London Media is a premium visual production agency working between London and Izmir. We make brand films, advertising, 3D visualization and drone cinematography for brands who treat an image as a promise.

/ Who we are

We are a small team of directors, cinematographers and 3D artists who care about one thing above all: the feeling a frame leaves behind.

From London and Izmir, we partner with brands across film, advertising and visualization. Two cities, one standard. We keep the team tight so the craft stays close, and we treat every project as a piece of cinema, whatever its length.

We have worked with Samsung, Karaca, LC Waikiki, Yeni Raki, Le Cordon Bleu and Barrus London, among others. From content to impact: that is the whole job.

/ Philosophy

Ideas move.
Quality shows.

World London Media · London & Izmir

Design, for us, is not decoration: it is conveying the right emotion, accurately. We do not fear whitespace. We treat animation as a tool, never a goal. Every choice of light, typeface and cut answers to one question, what should the viewer feel?

So we begin with the feeling, and build everything backward from there. Minimalist, cinematic, with room to breathe. From content to impact: a frame should be a promise the brand can keep.

How we work

Four steps, from idea to delivery.

01
Step 01

Discover

We listen first. We map your brand, your audience and the feeling you want to leave behind, then turn it into a single clear direction.

02
Step 02

Direct

We shape the look. Boards, references, light and movement come together into a treatment, so everyone sees the film before a single frame is shot.

03
Step 03

Produce

We shoot with the patience of cinema. Cinema lenses, the right crew and the time it takes to get the frame, not just a frame.

04
Step 04

Deliver

We finish in post: edit, color and sound, cut to every format you need. One cinematic master, tuned for film, digital and social.
