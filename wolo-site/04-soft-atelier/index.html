A studio note

The craft of moving people.

World London Media (WoLo) is a premium visual production studio working between London and Izmir. We turn brand ambition into film, advertising and design that audiences actually feel.

We treat animation as a tool, not a goal. Every frame, every cut, every render exists to carry the right emotion, accurately. That is the whole discipline.

· What we make

Six disciplines,
one obsession with quality.

  • i. Brand Films Cinematic brand storytelling. Narrative, light and pace shaped so your brand says something worth remembering. →
  • ii. Advertising Content for TV, digital and social. Campaigns built to perform, crafted so they never feel like an ad. →
  • iii. 3D Visualization Architectural renders and product modeling. Photoreal worlds and objects, built before they ever exist. →
  • iv. Corporate Film Corporate storytelling with warmth. Company films, interviews and culture pieces that respect the audience. →
  • v. Commercial Production Full commercial production. Concept, crew, locations and post, run end to end with one accountable team. →
  • vi. Drone Cinematography Aerial perspectives. Licensed drone work that opens a scene, reveals a scale, and earns the wide shot. →
