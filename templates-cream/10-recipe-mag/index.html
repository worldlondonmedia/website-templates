05 · The method

Four steps,
one good stew.

How we cook a project

4 weeks avg 06 humans + 3 cats 1 brief at a time
  1. i.

    The first conversation

    30-min call, no slides. We meet, walk through your space, eat your food, read the menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

  2. ii.

    Direction & preparation

    References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. Pinned to a wall, approved before any camera comes out of its case.

  3. iii.

    Principal photography

    Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

  4. iv.

    Edit & delivery

    Selects in 48 hours. Final masters within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately, archived indefinitely.
