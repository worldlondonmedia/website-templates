★ How we work

Four steps, one
very loose rhythm.

01.

Listen, mostly

30-min first call. We meet, walk the space, eat the food. No slides.

02.

Pin to wall

References, palette, casting, locations. Pinned to the cork board. Approved.

03.

Shoot slowly

Small crew, long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. No shortcuts.

04.

Edit & send

Selects in 48h. Final master in 10 working days. Archive forever.
