CONSTRUCTION PHASES

Four phases
to handover.

METHOD STATEMENT Schedule · Gantt Avg duration · 4 weeks

Briefing & discovery

30-minute first call, no slides. We meet, walk through your space, eat your food, read the menus. The brief writes itself.

DURATION — WEEK 01

Direction & drafting

References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. Pinned to a wall and approved before any camera comes out of its case.

DURATION — WEEK 02

Principal photography

Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

DURATION — WEEK 03

Edit & handover

Selects in 48h. Final masters within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately. Archive kept indefinitely.
