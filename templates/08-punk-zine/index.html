METHOD · FROM PITCH TO PRINT

Four movements,
one rhythm.

i.

The first conversation

30-min, no slides. We meet, walk the space, eat the food, read the menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

— WK 01
ii.

Direction & preparation

References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. Pinned to a wall, approved before any camera comes out.

— WK 02
iii.

Principal photography

Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

— WK 03
iv.

Edit & delivery

Selects in 48 hours. Final masters within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen, archived indefinitely.
