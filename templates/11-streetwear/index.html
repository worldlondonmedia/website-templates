— METHOD · FOUR MOVEMENTS —

Four movements,
one rhythm.

i.

The first conversation

A meeting, a walk through your atelier, a meal. The brief writes itself once we know the room and the people.

ii.

Direction & preparation

References, palette, casting, locations. Pinned to a wall, approved before any camera comes out of its case.

iii.

Principal photography

Small crews. Long lights. Real fabric, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

iv.

Edit & delivery

Selects in 48 hours. Final masters within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen, archived indefinitely.
