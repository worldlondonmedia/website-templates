§ How we work · on set

Our checklist, roughly,
before the camera comes out.

  • WEEK 01

    Listen, mostly

    30-minute first call, no slides. We meet, walk through your space, eat your food, read the old menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

    ↳ no agenda. no slides. tea.
  • WEEK 02

    Pin everything to the wall

    References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. Pinned to a literal cork board. Approved before any camera comes out of its bag.

    ↳ moodboards on actual paper.
  • WEEK 03

    Shoot slowly, on purpose

    Small crew, long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post. Bring snacks, leave nervous.

    ↳ avg crew size: 4 people.
  • WEEK 04

    Edit, grade, deliver

    Selects in 48 hours. Final delivery within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately. Archive kept indefinitely.

    ↳ archive lives forever.
