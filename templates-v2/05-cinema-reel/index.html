— Director's note

Cinema is just photography that won't sit still.

— S. Çağlayan, Director & Founder

I started this studio because I wanted brand work to feel like cinema. Not glossy advertising, not corporate film — the kind of moving image that takes its time, that loves its subject, that earns the right to stay in your head for a week.

We are six people. Some of us came from documentary, some from fashion, some from food. We meet on Mondays, we shoot on Thursdays, we edit on Sundays. Our reel is short on purpose. We are picky.

If you are a restaurant, a hotel, a small label, or simply someone who knows that good pictures matter, we'd love to hear from you.

— Production

Four phases.
One picture at a time.

PHASE 01

Pre-
production

Brief, references, casting, locations, schedule. We pin everything to a wall and approve before a camera comes out.

PHASE 02

Principal
photography

Small crews, long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

PHASE 03

Post
production

Edit, grade, sound, titles. We grade for paper and for screen separately. Selects in 48 hours, master in 10 working days.

PHASE 04

Release
& archive

Delivery in your DAM, your folders, your social calendar. Archive kept indefinitely. Re-licensed on request.
