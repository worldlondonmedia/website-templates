A studio note

On the patience of seeing.

Founded in a small room above a print shop in Clerkenwell — with a single ambition. To bring the patience of editorial photography to the impatience of brand work.

We work slowly, mostly. We light carefully. We cast for character before glamour. The result is imagery that holds its breath, and lasts longer than a season.

— What we make

A studio of five disciplines,
one obsession with light.

  • i. Photography Hospitality, food, and product. Lit like editorial — shot until the room remembers itself. →
  • ii. Film & motion Brand films, shorts, and the social cuts that don't feel like advertising. →
  • iii. Creative direction Concept, casting, locations, and the small cruel decisions that make a campaign feel inevitable. →
  • iv. Social editorial Monthly creative for brands who treat their feed as a magazine. Strategy through to publishing. →
  • v. Brand identity Visual systems built to hold for a decade. Type, palette, photographic codes — kept honest. →

— Method

Four movements,
one rhythm.

i.

Listen

We meet, walk through your space, eat your food, read the old menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

Week 01 · Discovery
ii.

Direct

References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. We pin it to a wall and approve it before a camera comes out.

Week 02 · Direction
iii.

Shoot

Small crew. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive.

Week 03 · Production
iv.

Deliver

Selects in 48 hours. Final delivery within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately.
