— Of the Method —

Four movements,
one rhythm.

❦ ❦ ❦

  • i.

    The first conversation

    A meeting, a walk through your space, a meal in your kitchen. The brief writes itself once we know the room and the people.

  • ii.

    Direction & preparation

    References, palette, casting, locations, schedule — pinned to the wall, approved before any camera comes out of its case.

  • iii.

    Principal photography

    Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

  • iv.

    Edit & delivery

    Selects in 48 hours. Final masters within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen, archived indefinitely.
