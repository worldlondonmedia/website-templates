How we work, roughly →

01 · Hi

The first call

30 minutes, no slides. Tell us the room, the food, the vibe, the budget. We say yes or no honestly.

02 · Plan

Mood & map

References, palette, casting, schedule. Pin everything to a wall. Approve before any camera comes out.

03 · Shoot

The fun part

Small crew, long days, real lighting. No "stock smile" energy. Bring snacks, leave nervous.

04 · Edit

The edit

Graded for paper and screen separately. Delivered in your DAM, your folders, your archive forever.

You're probably wondering →

How much does a project cost?

Half-day shoots start at £1,800. Full social packages from £3,200/month. We send proper quotes after the first call — no surprises, no agency markups.

Do you work outside London?

Often. We've shot in Istanbul, Lisbon, Paris and Mallorca this year. Travel is on us for projects over £8k.

Can I just hire you for a single shoot?

Absolutely. Most clients start that way. The retainer thing is optional — we don't push it.

Do you do social media management too?

We make the content. We schedule it. We don't reply to your DMs — that's your team. Strategy and rhythm we'll handle gladly.

What's your turnaround?

Edit selects in 48 hours. Final delivery within 10 working days for stills, 14 for film. Faster if you're paying us to be faster.
