— On craft

A photograph is the quietest sound a brand can make. We make it well.

Selin Çağlayan, Founder

The studio was founded in 2021 in a small room above a print shop in Clerkenwell, with a single ambition: to bring the patience of editorial photography to the impatience of brand work. We work slowly, mostly. We light carefully. We cast for character before glamour.

Today we work with restaurants, hotels, fashion houses, and the rare independent brand who still believes images should look like something. We are six people, three cats, and a studio that is, finally, large enough to stand back from the lens.

03.
Method

Four movements, one rhythm.

i. Discovery

Listening,
mostly.

We meet, we walk through your space, we eat your food, we read your old menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

ii. Direction

The mood
before the move.

We build a visual world — references, palettes, casting, locations — and pin it to the wall before any camera comes out of its bag.

iii. Production

Shoot days,
quietly intense.

Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

iv. Delivery

The edit
is the work.

We grade for paper and screen separately. We deliver everything you need, nothing you don't, and we keep your archive forever.
