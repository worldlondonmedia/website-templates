§ 005 Method
Of work

Four movements, one rhythm.

01

Listen.

30-minute first call, no slides. We meet, walk through your space, eat your food, read the old menus. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

→ Week 01
02

Direct.

References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. We pin everything to a wall and approve it before any camera comes out of its bag.

→ Week 02–03
03

Shoot.

Small crews. Long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

→ Week 03–04
04

Deliver.

Selects in 48 hours. Final delivery within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately. Archive kept forever.

→ Week 04–05
