section/process

Four steps to a final
master commit.

01.

discovery --listen

30-min first call, no slides. We meet, walk through your space, eat your food. The brief writes itself once we know the room.

→ status: ok / 1 week
02.

direction --pin-to-wall

References, palette, casting, locations, schedule. Pinned to the wall. Approved before any camera comes out of its bag.

→ status: ok / 2 weeks
03.

shoot --slowly --on-purpose

Small crew, long lights. Real food, real people, real rooms. We move slowly because mistakes get expensive in post.

→ status: ok / 1–2 weeks
04.

deploy --grade --archive

Selects in 48h. Final delivery within 10 working days. Graded for paper and screen separately. Archive kept indefinitely.

→ status: shipped / 10 days
